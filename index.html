About Music Play

Anyone can enter the Open Mic, so if you think you have the raw talent to impress industry professionals and if you’d like to receive feedback that’ll help you grow as an artist; we want to hear from you! All types of singers, singer/songwriters, rappers and vocal groups are welcome to audition for the music competition, and the aim is to discover and develop acts that have the ability to become a professional recording artist. Open Mic gives you the chance to develop your performance, vocal abilities and professionalism. So, whatever level of singer you are, we’ll provide valuable feedback and help you develop.

MORE THAN A MUSIC COMPETITION

Sometimes big dreams pay off. If you’ve ever dreamed of performing for thousands and winning a record deal, the annual Open Mic music competition — the biggest search for unsigned talent in the UK — is for you. Auditions for start soon - with more than 10,000 people entering each year, from singers and singer/songwriters to rappers and vocal groups. All genres are welcome at these music competitions, and acts can perform covers or original songs.

PERFORM FOR RECORD LABEL A&R

One of the toughest things about breaking into the music industry is getting a chance to perform in front of industry professionals. Music industry judges are at every stage of Open Mic UK's music competitions, from the auditions to the grand final.

BOOST YOUR CAREER

We're with our performers every step of the way, offering feedback from professionals, exclusive opportunities and resources even after the competition is over. Here is a selection of resources for artists even if you haven't joined the competition yet.

Our Music Management team are made up of industry experts who are passionate about supporting the next generation of talented artists on their journey of growth and success. We tailor the ongoing development with our management team to suit the needs of each individual artist, with sessions starting at 1 hour per month. You’ll have a dedicated artist development manager who will offer you guidance and mentorship, along with access to one-to-one sessions with specialist industry professionals. Being on our roster will also mean you will get exclusive sync representation and can benefit from our experienced music promotions team at special rates. Our team of dedicated artist managers have previously worked with the likes of The 1975, Young T & Bugsey, Joss Stone and Ghetts, as well as working for GRM Daily, Kerrang, Sony and Ministry of Sound… to name just a few!!

Pricing & Rights

We match our experienced managers to each artist, catering to individual development goals, and we have options for a range of budgets starting from £150 per month - so please make sure you speak with a member of our friendly team to find out more. Traditionally, a manager will take a commission on everything you earn both now and in the future - including stream revenue and performance fees. This commission is usually between 15% - 25%. They will also usually own a percentage of your rights. Our model is different - our managers don’t take a commission or any rights and instead receive a fee for their consultancy. You’ll still get to benefit from your manager’s knowledge and expertise, without losing any rights or having the complexities of a management contract As an example, if you earn £20,000 in a year as an artist, on the traditional model you could owe up to £5,000 to your manager on those earnings - plus future earnings too. With Music Gateway - you get to keep it all!
